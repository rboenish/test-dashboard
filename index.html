Data Sources

This dashboard displays historical climate data for the five largest cities in Washington State. The data is compiled from the following authoritative sources:

  • Primary NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI)
    Climate at a Glance - Monthly and annual temperature and precipitation data for U.S. cities and regions.
  • Primary NOAA Regional Climate Centers - Western Regional Climate Center
    wrcc.dri.edu - Historical climate summaries and station data for Western U.S. locations.
  • Secondary National Weather Service (NWS) Seattle
    weather.gov/sew - Local climate data and historical records for the Puget Sound region.
  • Reference Office of the Washington State Climatologist
    climate.washington.edu - Washington-specific climate analysis and historical trends.

Note: The data displayed in this dashboard is generated based on historical climate averages and patterns from these sources. For official climate records and research purposes, please consult the original data sources directly.
